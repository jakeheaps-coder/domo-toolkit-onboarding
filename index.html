What is the Domo Toolkit?

Everything You Need, One Platform

The toolkit turns Claude Code into a Domo-branded creative and engineering workstation. Every output follows Domo's design system automatically.

Built for Domo's Marketing and Growth teams, the toolkit bundles a 22KB design playbook, a semantic knowledge graph of Domo messaging, AI media generation, Figma integration, full Domo platform API access (150+ tools, 624+ endpoints), and role-based access control into a single system that auto-updates daily. Just talk to Claude and it knows your brand, your products, and your workflow.

Credential Protection
